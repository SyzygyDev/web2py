Shannon McCarty, Personal Trainer

I’ve worked with many different types of clients, from those new to exercise to triathletes. Qualities that make the coaching relationship with me a great match include:



Being Open Minded

Open minded to try diet and exercise routines outside your comfort zone – meaning you do not already have a “this is the best way to do it” mentality.

Gym Equipment

Having some type of weight training equipment available to you at a gym or at home such as dumbbells and barbells.

Willingness to Journal Progress

Ability and willingness to report on your food intake and workout progress on a weekly basis.

Desire to Change

Everyone is busy. Everyone has curveballs thrown their way in their lives. Despite that we carry on. Make sure you are ready to incorporate healthy living into your routine despite what life throws at you - I promise this isn’t hard to do once good habits are set.
